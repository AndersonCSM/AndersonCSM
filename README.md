# 💫 About Me

**What I am currently doing**  
I hold a Bachelor's degree in Science and Technology and I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at the Federal Rural University of the Semi-Arid Region (UFERSA).

**What I am studying**  
I am deepening my knowledge in Computer Engineering fundamentals and technologies, with a focus on software development and Data Science applications. My main interests include:

- Back-end development  
- APIs and systems architecture  
- Relational and non-relational databases  
- Data structures and algorithms  
- Computational modeling and simulation  
- Data analysis tools and libraries  

I am particularly focused on **Python** and **Java**, exploring everything from core programming concepts to applications in data analysis, automation, and systems development.

**Interests and personal notes**  
I have a strong interest in **computational simulation** and **robotics**. I am also a science fiction enthusiast and enjoy references like *The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy* — being prepared is always a good idea.

I enjoy strategy and resource-management games such as *Age of Empires II*, *Cities: Skylines*, *Against the Storm*, and *Anno 1800*. Futuristic themes, especially within the **cyberpunk** genre, are a major source of inspiration that motivates me to keep studying and contributing to technological progress.
